Chemical Bonds

There are two main types of chemical bonds

  • Covalent Bond
  • Ionic Bond









Covalent Bonds

A Covalent bond , also known as a molecular bond, is formed when two or more atoms share electrons.
There is 2 main types of covalent bonds.

  1. Polar covalent bond
  2. Non Polar covalent bond

Polar Covalent Bond

Forms when electronegaivity values are a little further apart

Nonpolar Covalent Bond

forms when electronegaivity values are very similar

Ionic bonds

An Ionic bond is formed when one atom accepts or donates
one or more valence electrons , outer layer of the atom ,
to another electron

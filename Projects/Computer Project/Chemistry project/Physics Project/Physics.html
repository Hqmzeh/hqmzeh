Torque and seasaw

Toque is the product of mass and radius

The torque of each child about the pivot point is the product of the child's weight and the distance of the child (strictly speaking, the child's center of mass) from the pivot. The sign of the torque is positive if it would cause a counterclockwise rotation of the seesaw.












Without torque there will not be seasaw or even motors, engines, airplanes, wheels,etc
